# Getting Support

We're here to help! Here's how you can get support for the Knowledge Base project.

## Before You Ask

1. **Check the [Documentation](README.md)** - Your question might already be answered in our documentation.
2. **Search Issues** - Someone might have already reported a similar issue.
3. **Check [FAQs](FAQ.md)** - Common questions and their answers.

## How to Get Help

### GitHub Issues
For bug reports and feature requests, please [open an issue](https://github.com/yourusername/knowledge-base/issues/new/choose).

### Discussion Forum
Join our [Discussions](https://github.com/yourusername/knowledge-base/discussions) to ask questions and share ideas.

### Real-time Chat
For real-time help, join our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/your-invite-link).

## What to Include

When asking for help, please provide:

1. **Description**: Clear explanation of the issue
2. **Steps to Reproduce**: How to recreate the problem
3. **Expected vs Actual Behavior**: What you expected vs what happened
4. **Environment**: OS, browser, version, etc.
5. **Screenshots/Logs**: If applicable

## Response Time

We aim to respond to all support requests within 2 business days. For critical issues, please label them as such.

## Community Support

Our community is active and helpful. Don't hesitate to ask questions in the forums or chat.
